Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026

AnkaheAlfaaz is a platform for creating and sharing personal cinematic experiences. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. What We Collect

When you create an experience on AnkaheAlfaaz, we collect:

  • The sender's name and receiver's name (as entered by you)
  • The message content you write or generate
  • The occasion, theme, and feeling tags you select
  • Your UPI ID (only if you register as a reseller)
  • Basic technical data — browser type, approximate location, device type — for security and performance purposes only

We do not collect your phone number, government ID, financial card details, or any sensitive personal information beyond what is listed above.

2. How Long We Store Your Data

  • Free experiences — deleted entirely from our database 7 days after creation. The link expires in 24 hours.
  • Premium experiences — the link remains permanently active. The underlying message content is retained for 21 days for operational and support purposes, after which the content is wiped from our records. The experience link continues to function.
  • Reseller records — retained indefinitely for financial and legal compliance purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Deliver the experience you created to its intended recipient
  • Investigate reported or flagged experiences for safety purposes
  • Process reseller commission payments
  • Improve the platform's performance and reliability

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing. We do not read your messages unless they are reported to us.

4. Third-Party Services

AnkaheAlfaaz uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database storage. Your experience data is stored on Supabase servers.
  • Netlify — website hosting and delivery.
  • Groq — AI letter generation (premium feature). When you use AI generation, your sender name, receiver name, occasion, and feeling tags are sent to Groq's API. No message content is sent unless you have already written something and request transformation.

Each of these services maintains its own privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their policies if you have concerns.

5. Data Security

All data is stored on secured, encrypted servers. Access to experience content is restricted and is not accessed by our team unless a report is filed. We implement rate limiting, input sanitization, and access controls to protect your data from unauthorized use.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any experience you created by contacting us
  • Request information about what data we hold related to you
  • Report an experience you received that you found harmful or distressing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mayank023024@gmail.com.

7. Minors

AnkaheAlfaaz is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the platform, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update. Continued use of AnkaheAlfaaz after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, write to us at mayank023024@gmail.com. We respond within 72 hours.
